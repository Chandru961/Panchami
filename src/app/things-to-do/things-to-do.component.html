Things To Do In Bangalore

Visit Cubbon Park and Lal Bagh

Cubbon Park and Lal Bagh are two of the must-visit tourist places in Bangalore. Located in the heart of Bangalore, the lush and beautiful Cubbon Park is the ideal place for jogging and leisurely walks. Lal Bagh, one of the major Bangalore attractions, is a botanical garden covering 240 acres, dotted with about 1,854 species of plants.

Enjoy a wildlife safari at Bannerghatta National Park

A wildlife safari at Bannerghatta National Park is a must if you are visiting Bangalore for an extended stay. The zoo, crocodile farm and butterfly park located within the national park are its main attractions.

Have fun in Wonderla Amusement Park

Wonderla Amusement Park, Bangalore is located just an hour and a half away from Panchami Comforts via road. Wonderla is the perfect destination to have a fun-filled time with friends and family!
